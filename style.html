Amazon is a large American multinational technology company that started as an online bookstore in 1994 and grew into an e-commerce giant offering a vast selection of products online. It is also a major player in other sectors, including cloud computing through Amazon Web Services (AWS), digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. E-commerce: Amazon's core business is its online marketplace where consumers can buy a wide variety of goods, from electronics and books to groceries and clothing. Amazon Web Services (AWS): The company's cloud computing division is a leading provider of essential infrastructure for many online businesses and services. Digital Streaming: Amazon offers a variety of streaming services, including Prime Video for movies and TV shows and Amazon Music for music and podcasts. Artificial Intelligence: Amazon develops and integrates artificial intelligence into its products and services, such as Alexa. Other Ventures: The company has expanded into numerous other areas through subsidiaries like Whole Foods Market, Twitch, and Ring.
